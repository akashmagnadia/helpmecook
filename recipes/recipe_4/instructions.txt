Wash the Okra that will be cooked and put them on a napkin to let them dry.
Next, depending on your preference wash and cut the potato into pieces as you see it in the picture.
As you cut the potato, directly put them in the pan.
For each okra, remove small bit from both ends and for the middle portion cut them into small pieces.
As you cut the Okra, directly put them in the pan.
The pan on the stove and set the stove to medium heat.
Add 5 teaspoons of oil in the pan.
Don't stir it and just cover the pan with a lid.
After 5 minutes open the lid.
Add 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric and 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
Stir everything well and then close the lid.
After 10 minutes open the lid and stir it well adn then close the lid.
Open the lid and check if the stickiness of the okra is gone. Once that happens, we will add the spices.
Taste the potato and check the saltiness and add if needed.
Taste the okra to make sure it's more crisp than chewy.
If they are not cooked then wait until potato is soft and okra are crisp.
Add the following things inside the pan: 1/4 teaspoon chili paste (Fine Crushed Ginger, Garlic, Jalapenos), 1 teaspoon Coriander powder (Dhana Jeeru), 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder, 1/2 teaspoon lemon.
Stir the spices well and then close the lid.
Let it cook for 5 minutes and then open the lid.
Stir everything and then after 2 minutes turn off the stove.
