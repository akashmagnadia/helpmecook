Get a bowl to add 1/4 cup of Moong Dal and Chana Dal with enough water to soak the dal.
Set the instant pot and put it on sauté at low temperature for 10 minutes.
Add 3 teaspoons of oil to the instant pot.
After the oil is warmed up, add 1/4 teaspoon of mustard seeds.
After mustang seeds start to crackle, then add 1/4 teaspoon of whole cumin.
Add 1 pinch hing to the instant pot.
Chop 1 medium size onion, and add it to the instant pot.
Add 3 teaspoons of Ghee.
Add 2 pinch of secret masala.
Chop 1 big tomato or 2 small tomatoes.
Sauté the onion until they turn brown or orange.
Add 1/2 teaspoon of green chilli paste and let it cook for 2 minutes.
Add 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric to the instant pot.
1/2 teaspoon of red chilli powder to the instant pot.
1 teaspoon of coriander powder to the instant pot.
1/4 teaspoon of Garam Masala to the instant pot.
1/4 teaspoon of Kasturi Methi to the instant pot.
1/2 teaspoon of Salt to the instant pot.
Add chopped tomato to the instant pot and let it cook until the water doesn't evaporate from the tomatoes.
Wash both of the dals, rinse all the water, and then add it to the instant pot.
Add 2.5 cups of water to the instant pot.
Add 1 teaspoon of Ghee to the instant pot.
If you want to also cook Jeera Rice along with Dal Fry, follow Jeera Rice recipe.
Set the instant pot setting on low pressure for 20 minutes.
After you open the instant pot, add fresh coriander if you have them available.
