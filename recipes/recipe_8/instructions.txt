If you are making Cabbage, wash it first and then chop it into pieces while avoiding the root.
If you are making Cauliflower, then chop it into big pieces while avoiding the root and then wash it.
Peel and chop the potatoes into cube-like-size, which will be needed next.
Set a pan on the stove for medium heat and add 5 teaspoons of Oil to the pan.
Add mustard seeds to the pan and turn off the stove when they start to crackle.
Wait 2 minutes for the oil to cool down.
Add the following things to the oil in the pan: 1/4 teaspoon of Turmeric powder, 1/2 teaspoon Salt, 1/2 teaspoon of chili paste (Fine Crushed Ginger, Garlic, Jalapenos)
Mix it well.
Add both the chopped vegetables to the pan and mix it well.
Cover the pan with a lid and set the stove to medium heat.
After 7 minutes open the lid, mix it well, and cover it again.
After 5 minutes mix it again and check the softness of the potato and salt. Adjust accordingly.
After the potatoes are half-cooked, add the following spices to the pan.
1 teaspoon coriander powder, 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli, 1/4 teaspoon Pav Bhaji masala
Chop one medium tomato and add it to the pan, but don't mix it.
Add chopped Fresh coriander (Dhana) if available.
Cook for about 3 minutes and mix well.
After 2 minutes turn off the stove, if everything is cooked.
