This dish is made for one meal, change accordingly.
Wash 1 cup Jasmin Rice and 1/2 cup Toor Dal.
Put following things in the instant pot: Washed 1 cup Jasmin Rice and 1/2 cup Toor Dal, 1/4 teaspoon turmeric, 1 teaspoon salt, 4 cup water, 2 teaspoon Ghee
Close the lid of the instant pot and cook it for 20 minutes on a high-pressure setting.
