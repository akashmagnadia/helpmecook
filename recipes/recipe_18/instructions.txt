First, get tall pan for cooking pasta.
Put 1 teaspoon salt and half tablespoon oil in the water in the pan.
Heat up the water and when the water is hot, add 1 cup pasta.
Once the pasta is set to cook...
Get a large pan and put it on medium heat.
Get 4 cloves Garlic and cut into small pieces.
Before putting it in pan, cut 1 medium Onion and have it ready to put it in oil.
Put 1/4 cup butter and chopped garlic in the pan.
After you start smelling the garlic, put 4 tablespoon Olive Oil and chopped onion in the pan.
