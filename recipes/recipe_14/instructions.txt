Get an instant pot and about 12 small sized potatoes.
Add water in the instant pot and cook the potatoes on low heat for 20 minutes.
After they have been cooked, peel the skin off and cut them in half or bite-size pieces.
Get a large flat pan and add about 3 teaspoon oil.
After the oil is heated, add 1/2 teaspoon sesame seed.
After the sesame seed has bubbled/cooked, add potatoes in it.
Then add 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
Wait for 10 minutes.
Add 1.5 teaspoon Coriander powder (Dhana Jeeru).
Add 1 teaspoon Red Chilli Spice.
Add 1/4 teaspoon Black Salt.
Add juice from 1 lemon.
Stir, check spice level and let it cook until potatoes are soft and little crispy.
