Grab a small pot and put 3 teaspoons of oil.
Put it on the stove and set it to medium heat after which let the oil warm for about 3 minutes.
Then put 1/4 teaspoon of Mustard Seeds (Raay).
Once the Mustard Seeds start to crackle or dance, turn off the stove and wait for 2 minutes to cool down.
Put 1 pinch of Asafoetida (Hing) and 1/4 teaspoon of the red chilli powder into the oil.
Mix the spices with the oil.
Get a small plate or bowl to gather the following spices for the Dal.
1/2 teaspoon chili paste (Fine Crushed Ginger, Garlic, Jalapenos), 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric (Haldal), 1/2 teaspoon Salt, 1/4 teaspoon Sambhar Masala, 4 teaspoon sugar, 10 peanuts.
Add the prepared Dal inside the pot and then add the gathered spices inside the pot.
Put 1/2 teaspoon of lemon juice in the Dal.
Turn on the stove to medium heat and let it boil for 6 to 7 minutes while stirring it every 2 to 3 minutes.
Turn off the stove and add chopped Fresh coriander (Dhana) if available.
