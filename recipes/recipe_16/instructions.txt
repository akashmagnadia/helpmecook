Cut 1 medium onion and mix it in a mixer.
Cut 1/2 medium pepper and mix it in a mixer.
Get a large pan and out oil it in it and let it heat up.
After it has heat up stir in the mixed onion and bell pepper in the pan.
Cut 3 medium tomatoes and leave it in a dish for future.
Mix the following spices in the pan:
1 teaspoon Green Chilli paste
1/4 teaspoon Turmeric
1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli spice
1 teaspoon Coriander powder
1/4 teaspoon Garam Masala
1/2 teaspoon salt
Mix in the tomatoes, set the stove to low and stir it until the water has burned
Mix either 4 eggs or shredded paneer.
Garnish with cilantro.
