Wash and peel the potatoes.
After slicing them from the middle, cut them so that they are long as seen in the picture.
Put the pan on the stove and set the stove to medium heat.
Add 5 teaspoons of oil to the pan.
Add 1/2 teaspoon of cumin powder (Jiru) and turn off the stove after they get puffy or start to crackle.
Wait 2 minutes for the oil to cool down.
Add 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric, 1/4 teaspoon of salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of chili paste (Fine Crushed Ginger, Garlic, Jalapenos) to the pan.
Then add the sliced potatoes to the pan and stir it well and then close the pan with a lid.
After 5 minutes open the lid, stir it, and then cover the pan with a lid.
After 5 minutes check if the potatoes are half soft and the salt level is good. Adjust accordingly.
If they are half-cooked then more spices are ready to be added.
Add the following things inside the pot: 1 teaspoon of Coriander powder (Dhana Jeeru), and 1/4 teaspoon of Red Chilli powder.
Then stir it well and cover the pan with a lid.
Let it cook for 5 minutes.
If the potato is soft then it's ready, otherwise, let it cook for as much time as needed.
Turn off the stove and add chopped Fresh coriander (Dhana) and 1/2 teaspoon of lemon juice if available.
