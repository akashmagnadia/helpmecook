Get a large flat pan and set the heat to medium.
Put 2 tablespoon of oil in the pan.
Get Tofu, drain all the water from it and a paper napkin to get it as dry as possible.
Cut the Tofu into cubes or rectangles.
Let the Tofu get little crisp but still stay soft in the middle and turn it occasionally to cook all the sides.
Add 1 teaspoon Salt to the Tofu.
Cut 1 diced pepper and 1 long piece onion while Tofu is cooking.
After tofu is cooked, remove it from the pan and toss in vegetables and handful Spring Onion.
Create a sauce with the following things in a bowl:
1.5 teaspoon green chilli paste.
2 tablespoon Soy Sauce
3.5 tablespoon Red Chilli Sauce
3 spoon Ketchup
2 teaspoon sugar
1/2 Lime
1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder
Use 1 tablespoon water to clean out the bowl
