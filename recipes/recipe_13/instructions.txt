Get a large flat pan and set the heat to medium.
Put 2 tablespoon of oil in the pan.
Get 1 pound of Tofu and drain all the water from the Tofu.
Cut the Tofu into cubes or rectangles.
Then place them on a paper napkin to get it as dry as possible.
Get a container in which you can mix the Tofu and Spices.
Put Tofu, 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli and 1 teaspoon Salt and mix it well.
Then put all mixed item in the pan.
Let the Tofu get little crisp and turn it occasionally to cook all the sides.
Add 1 teaspoon green chilli paste, 3 spoon Soy Sauce, 3.5 spoon Red Chilli Sauce, and 2 spoon Ketchup in the pan.
While Tofu is being cooked, cut 2 peppers and 1 onion and then toss them in the pan.
Let it cook until peppers and onions are soft.
