Cut 3 medium tomatoes and leave it in a dish for future.
Get a pan and out oil it in it and let it heat up.
Mix the following spices in the pan together:
1 teaspoon Green Chilli paste
1/4 teaspoon Turmeric
1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli spice
1 teaspoon Coriander powder
1 teaspoon Cumin powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
Mix in the tomatoes, set the stove to medium and stir it until it's hot.
Add Sev at the end to garnish.
