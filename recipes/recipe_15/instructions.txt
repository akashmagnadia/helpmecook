Get 2 cup of sugar and 1 2/3 cup water and place it in a medium soup pan.
Boil the water and then bring the water to room temperature.
Get 5 medium pieces of ginger and remove the skin.
Cut into small pieces and crush them using the mixer.
Mix it with following items:
1 cup lemon juice
1 teaspoon black salt
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
4 teaspoon cumin powder
