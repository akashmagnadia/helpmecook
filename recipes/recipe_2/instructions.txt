This recipe assumes that you have 1/2 cup dal pressure cooked with one tomato.
Chop the onion into small pieces.
Grab a small pot and put 3 teaspoons of oil.
Put it on the stove and set it to medium heat after which let the oil warm for about 3 minutes.
Add the chopped onion to the pot.
Set the stove to low heat now.
Get a small plate or bowl to gather the following spices for the Dal.
1/2 teaspoon chili paste (Fine Crushed Ginger, Garlic, Jalapenos), 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric (Haldal), 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder, 1 teaspoon Coriander powder (Dhana Jeeru), 1/2 teaspoon Salt, 1/4 teaspoon Punjabi Masala.
Turn off the stove for 1 minute to let the oil cool down and then add the gathered spices.
Immediately, add the prepared Dal inside the pot.
Turn on the stove to medium heat.
Put 1/2 teaspoon of lemon juice.
Let the Dal boil for 6 to 7 minutes while stirring it occasionally.
Turn off the stove and add chopped Mint (Pudina) if available.
