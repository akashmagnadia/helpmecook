Get a plate or dish to gather the following ingredients:
2 teaspoons all-purpose flour (Mendo), 1/4 teaspoon red chilli powder, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder, 1/4 teaspoon onion powder, 1/4 teaspoon of cumin (Jeeru), 1/4 teaspoon Oregano, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1/4 teaspoon salt.
Open a can of 14 oz diced tomatoes.
Get a small pan and pour 3 teaspoons of oil.
Turn the stove on to medium and let it heat up for a minute.
Then saut√© only all-purpose flour for 30 seconds.
Add the remaining spices to the pan.
After about 20 seconds, pour the canned diced tomatoes inside the pan.
Add 1/2 cup water to the pan.
Turn off the stove.
Using a hand blender, well blend everything in the pan. 
Set the stove to medium heat.
Stir the contents of the pan occasionally to not let the flour burn.
Keep stirring until the sauce becomes thick and starts to boil.
Once that happens the sauce is ready to be served.
